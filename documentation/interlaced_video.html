Interlaced video

Back to main page

Many of the cameras will record interlaced videos.

This is fine for tracking but causes problems with calibration as individual frames can appear "combed" or "torn". For example

This can cause the chessboard detection algorithm to fail or mess up the calibration even if the chessboard is successfully detected.

You can de-interlace videos with HandBrake.

Alternatively you can use ffmpeg directly:

$ ffmpeg -i input.file -vf yadif output.file
